Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

JEDlabs (the “Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, textScope (the “Site”), a utility tool for topic categorization and text analysis.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information. However, we use session cookies to manage temporary user-created topics. These cookies are stored locally in your browser and expire after about an hour.

2. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the Site. Specifically, we use cookies for:

  • Session Management: To allow users to create and use temporary topics during their session.
  • Analytics (if applicable): If we integrate third-party tools like Google Analytics in the future, they may use cookies to track usage patterns.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not currently use third-party services, we may in the future (e.g., Google Ads or Google Analytics). When this occurs, our Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect how those services process user data.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any user-uploaded data. All uploaded files and user inputs are processed in the browser and cleared after the session ends.

5. User Rights

If you are located in certain regions (e.g., the EU under the GDPR or California under the CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Request information about the cookies used.
  • Withdraw consent for cookies via your browser settings.

6. Cookies & Consent Management

Upon your first visit, a cookie consent banner will appear (if required by law) to inform you of our cookie usage and allow you to manage your preferences.

7. Contact Information

For questions, please contact us via our contact form.
